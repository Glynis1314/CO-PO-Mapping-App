# DBIT SO–PO Attainment System – Academic Structure

## 1. Roles
- Principal
- HOD
- Subject Teacher

## 2. Academic Year
Format:
- YYYY–YY ODD
- YYYY–YY EVEN
Example:
- 2025–26 ODD
- 2025–26 EVEN

## 3. Department
Example:
- Computer Engineering
- Information Technology

## 4. Programme Outcomes (PO)
PO1 to PO12 (as per NBA)

## 5. Programme Specific Outcomes (PSO)
PSO1, PSO2, PSO3

## 6. Course Outcomes (CO)
Each subject has:
- CO1, CO2, CO3, CO4, CO5

## 7. CO–PO Mapping Levels
- 3 → High
- 2 → Medium
- 1 → Low

## 8. CO Attainment Targets
Example:
- Expected Proficiency: 60%
- Level 3: 85% students ≥ proficiency
- Level 2: 70% students ≥ proficiency
- Level 1: 60% students ≥ proficiency

## 9. Attainment Methods
- Direct Attainment (CIA + SEE)
- Method B (Average-based)
- Indirect Attainment (Course Exit Survey – later)

## 10. Evidence
- CSV Marks
- Assessment Papers
- Rubrics
- Reports
