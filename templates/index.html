Welcome to DBIT Attainment System (Prototype)

Quick demo flows for HOD, Teachers and Principal. Use the cards to jump into the relevant dashboard pages. Later these will be protected by real authentication and connected to the Django backend models.

  • Create academic years (e.g., 2025-26 ODD/EVEN)
  • Assign subjects to teachers
  • Teachers create assessments and map questions → COs
  • Upload marks (CSV/Excel) and calculate CO / PO attainment
  • Upload and store evidence files for audits and presentations
